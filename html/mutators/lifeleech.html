Mutator: Life Leech

Enemy units steal life or shields whenever they do damage.

Difficulty points1
Available in customYes
Rollable in Chaos StudiosYes

Detailed Mutator Information

Life Leech heals Amon's units for 100% of the damage they do against player units.

Mutator Interactions

Aggressive DeploymentUnits from Aggressive Deployment Drops have the Life Leech buff.
Alien IncubationBroodlings have Life Leech.
DiffusionDamage dealt by Diffusion counts as Life Leech for the unit attacked.
Double EdgedDamage dealt by Double Edged does not trigger Life Leech.
Eminent DomainTurrets captured by Amon have Life Leech.
Gift ExchangeUnits spawned from Gift Exchange have Life Leech.
Heroes from the StormAmon Heroes have Life Leech.
Just DieRespawned units have Life Leech.
Laser DrillLaser Drill has Life Leech.
OutbreakInfested and Aberrations have Life Leech.
TransmutationTransmuted units have Life Leech.
Trick or TreatUnits spawned from Civilians have Life Leech.
Turkey ShootTurkeys have Life Leech.
Void ReanimatorsResurrected units have Life Leech.
Void RiftsUnits spawned from Void Rifts have Life Leech.
Walking InfestedInfested have Life Leech.
