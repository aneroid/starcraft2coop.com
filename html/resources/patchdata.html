Game Core (5.0.5) 2020-12-02

  • Commander Changes:
    • Zagara
      • Mastery sets have been fixed and now work correctly after games.

Chain of Ascension (v.177) 2020-02-24

  • Ji'nara will now have voice lines if player's forces or Amon's forces get destroyed around her.

Cradle of Death (v.1854) 2020-02-24

  • Changed how player units are removed from the game during the Victory sequence.
  • Modified regular Transmission to Victory Tranmission on mission Victory.

Dead of Night (v.873) 2020-02-24

  • Fixed a bug where a ping marker would remain active even after the Special Infested was killed.

Lock & Load (v.261) 2020-02-24

  • Internal changes to Lock Attack Wave triggers to handle spawn-camping of attack waves.

Malwarfare (v.8316) 2020-02-24

  • Changed ownership of Bonus Objectives to a different Player number.
  • Fixed a bug that would cause a spawn-camped Suppression Tower to not trigger its death events.
  • Modified regular Transmission to Victory Tranmission on mission Victory.

Miner Evacuation (v.627) 2020-02-24

  • Internal changes for unhandled errors if one of the Eradicators were killed off.
  • Modified regular Transmission to Victory Tranmission on mission Victory.

Mist Opportunities (v.311) 2020-02-24

  • Fixed a bug which would cause Terrazine Harvesting Bots to get stuck before they started moving towards Terrazine Geysers.
  • Modified regular Transmission to Victory Tranmission on mission Victory.

Oblivion Express (v.363) 2020-02-24

  • Fixed a bug where Train Cars would not spawn correctly.
  • Modified regular Transmission to Victory Tranmission on mission Victory.

Part and Parcel (v.1007) 2020-02-24

  • Fixed a bug where Train Cars would not spawn correctly.
  • Fixed a bug where attack waves would not spawn in the correct locaion.
  • Modified regular Transmission to Victory Tranmission on mission Victory.

Rifts to Korhal (v.311) 2020-02-24

  • Modified regular Transmission to Victory Tranmission on mission Victory.

Scythe of Amon (v.182) 2020-02-24

  • Killing Hatcheries, Lairs and Hives around the map no longer automatically kills the larva around them.
  • Modified regular Transmission to Victory Tranmission on mission Victory.

Temple of the Past (v.155) 2020-02-24

  • Void Thrasher spawn triggers now use Mission Time instead of AI Time.
  • Modified regular Transmission to Victory Tranmission on mission Victory.

The Vermillion Problem (v.273) 2020-02-24

  • Internal changes for unhandled errors if Molten Sal were being spawn-camped.
  • Modified regular Transmission to Victory Tranmission on mission Victory.

Void Launch (v.231) 2020-02-24

  • Modified regular Transmission to Victory Tranmission on mission Victory.

Void Thrashing (v.501) 2020-02-24

  • Modified regular Transmission to Victory Tranmission on mission Victory.

Note: All data generated on this site is based on Brutal difficulty. Timings, compositions and other elements may differ on easier difficulty levels.
